Server Rules

These rules apply to all players on moesmp.net. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

1. General Conduct

  • Respect All Players: No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination
  • Appropriate Language: Keep chat clean and family-friendly
  • No Spamming: Avoid excessive messages, commands, or chat flooding
  • English Only: Main chat must be in English for moderation purposes
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services

2. Gameplay Rules

  • No Griefing: Do not destroy or modify others' builds without permission
  • No Stealing: Respect other players' items and builds
  • No Cheating: Any form of cheating or exploiting is prohibited
  • No Hacked Clients: Only use official Minecraft clients
  • Fair PvP: Follow PvP rules in designated areas only
  • No Lag Machines: Do not create intentional server lag

3. Building Guidelines

  • Appropriate Builds: No offensive, inappropriate, or explicit structures
  • Respect Spawn: Keep spawn area clean and accessible
  • No Excessive Entities: Limit mob farms and item frames
  • Claim Your Land: Use protection plugins to secure your builds

4. Account Responsibility

  • Your Account: You are responsible for all actions on your account
  • No Account Sharing: Do not share your account with others
  • One Account Per Player: Multiple accounts are not allowed
  • No Impersonation: Do not impersonate staff or other players

5. Consequences

Violations may result in:

Violation Level First Offense Repeat Offense Severe Violation
Minor Warning 1-3 day ban 7 day ban
Moderate 1-3 day ban 7-14 day ban 30 day ban
Major 7-30 day ban Permanent ban Permanent IP ban

6. Appeal Process

If you believe you were unfairly punished:

  1. Join our Discord server
  2. Submit an appeal in the appropriate channel
  3. Provide your Minecraft username and ban details
  4. Explain why you believe the punishment was unfair
  5. Wait for staff review (typically 3-7 days)
